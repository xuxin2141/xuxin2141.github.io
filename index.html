Xu Xin is a Ph.D. student at Tianjin University of Science & Technology. He has published 6 SCI papers with 9 first-author citations, including 2 in CAS Zone 1 Top journals (Food Chemistry, IF 9.8; Journal of Cleaner Production, IF 10.0), plus additional journal articles and conference presentations. He has been recognized as an Outstanding Graduate Student (2024) and awarded the Outstanding Master's Thesis (2025). His research focuses on computer-vision-based intelligent drying systems for agricultural products.
